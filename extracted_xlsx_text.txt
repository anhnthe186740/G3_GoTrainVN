Structures physics-based train compositions (e.g., SE1, TN2 models) and maps layout distributions (Hard seat, soft seat, sleeper cabin matrices).

Live Itinerary Scheduling
Schedules precise calendar itineraries, configuring absolute arrival, waiting, and departure time allocations per intermediate transit hub.
Cấu hình ma trận Chính sách giá vé (Pricing Configuration)
Dynamic Pricing & Rates Admin
Establishes pricing formulas based on base fare rates per kilometer (basePricePerKm), seat multipliers, and specialized passenger category discount factors.

Campaigns & Vouchers Admin
Configures coupon logic including structural deduction values, budget limits, expirations, and minimum order thresholds for targeted promotional campaigns.

User Accounts & RBAC Controls
Monitors user indexes and executes administrative account actions (Activate / Suspend) across employee profiles (Staff) and registered customers (User).

Compiles real-time order ledgers into business intelligence reports and interactive revenue charts filterable by calendar timelines or specific train designations.

Capacity Optimization Tools
Renders interactive seat occupancy heatmaps across segment combinations to empower administrative vehicle allocation and dynamic inventory management.

Security & System Compliance
Tracks comprehensive chronological ledger footprints capturing system changes and live delay logs dispatched by the Staff crew for platform accountability.

Manages fixed percentage discounts and regulatory eligibility rules for distinct passenger archetypes (e.g., Students, Children, Seniors).

Displays a personalized account dashboard with greeting, booking statistics, upcoming trip summary, booking history, wallet balance, loyalty points, and quick actions such as Book Ticket, View Profile, Search Ticket, and Cancel Ticket.
